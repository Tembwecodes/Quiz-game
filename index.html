Game Instructions

The goal of the game is to answer all questions correctly to earn full points at the end of the game and win. The player reads the question and assesses the options of answers given. Then the player picks the correct answer and waits for the next question to load. The game continues until all questions have been asked. The player is then shown the score at the end of the game and is declared winner or loser. The game includes a variety of questions with multiple choice open-ended.
